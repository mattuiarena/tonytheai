How Tony AI Works

Connect, Sync, Assist

Tony AI is your web-based assistant that connects all your home health management tools, syncs data, and delivers real-time insights and excellent guidance for agency operations through a chat interface. From patient care to billing, Tony simplifies it all, ensuring your agency runs smoothly.

Connect Your Tools

Connecting Tony AI is easy. Share your credentials for scheduling, billing, and more, and our team handles the setup. Prefer DIY? Our simple tools let you do it yourself. Seamless integration, so you can focus on what matters.

Sync and Centralize

Tony AI keeps your data in sync, from scheduling to billing, always up-to-date and accurate. Think of it as your proactive partner, ensuring your agency runs smoothly with all the data and assistance at your fingertips.

Analyze and Assist

Tony AI delivers sharp insights for smarter decisions via our web and mobile app. Boost efficiency, enhance patient care, and grow your agency with guidance that drives revenue, so you can focus on what truly matters—exceptional care.
